About Us

Sakura Rapolu : Liza Moody : Nicole Rocha

We are Girls Who Code students at Twitter who came together to create an algorithm that summarizes documents. It analyzes an article and returns the main points. In a world filled with busy schedules and fake news, it is important that everyone stays informed. We hope that our tool will help people obtain briefs update on what is happening in the world and allow them to form their own opinions.
